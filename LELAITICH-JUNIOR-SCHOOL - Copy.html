About Lelaitich Junior School

Lelaitich Junior School is a modern junior secondary school offering Grade 7, Grade 8, and Grade 9 ONLY under the CBC curriculum. We focus on academic excellence, discipline, and learner welfare.

School Components

Grade 7

Foundation for junior secondary learning.

Grade 8

Skill development and learner growth.

Grade 9

Preparation for senior secondary pathways.

This institution operates ONLY Grades 7, 8, and 9.
